About Me
Siddiqa Kashaf
AI Chatbot Developer | Generative AI Enthusiast

I am a passionate AI Chatbot Developer and Generative AI enthusiast, holding a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). My journey in technology is driven by a fascination with artificial intelligence and its potential to transform how businesses interact with their customers.

My project experience centers around building Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) based customer support chatbot systems. I leverage technologies like Python, FastAPI, LangChain, Ollama, and PostgreSQL to create intelligent solutions that understand and respond to user queries in natural language.

I am deeply passionate about Generative AI, especially Large Language Models (LLMs), and enjoy solving real-world automation problems. My goal is to build AI-driven backend systems that are not only efficient but also scalable and user-friendly.
